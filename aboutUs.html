COMPANY PROFILE

Gosure Brokers cc was formed in March 1990.

The company grew by providing personal and efficient service to personal and business clientele.

In October 1996 the company moved to new premises in South Beach Avenue, Durban.

In April 1998, the CC was forced to move to larger premises to accommodate increase in staff numbers.

Our motto is “Our priority is to assess your risk” , and we adhere to the principle of viable catastrophe cover which is in keeping with our philosophy, leading us to advise potential customers to cover substantial catastrophe losses, which would have crippling financial burden on the resources of the company, and may affect its future sustainability.

Our Company is managed by Ango P. Saminathan and five full time staff that are fully trained and qualified to take care of the day to day work.

Our social responsibility is varied, in that, we assist NGOs and actively part take in developing students and children. We are active members of Human Value Advocacy Group – an organisation which offers free training.
